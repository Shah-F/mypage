Student Seminar on Symplectic Geometry

HU-students Seminar on Symplectic Geometry

We aim to cover the contents of Lectures on Symplectic Geometry by Ana Cannas da Silva. This could also act as a prerequisite to advanced symplectic events that happen at HU. The All of our events will happen virtually.
This seminar is organised by students for students. If you would like to attend or help with organising or give a lecture on some topics of your choice from the lecture notes please write to us on symplecticg@gmail.com.

    Some prerequisites:
  • Smooth manifolds, Vector fields and their flows, Basics Riemannian geometry: Chapters in Differential Geometry: Connections, Curvature, and Characteristic Classes by Loring.
  • Differential Forms, De Rham Cohomology: Chapter 5, 6 in Introduction to differential topology by Salamon and Robbin.
  • Homology and cohomology: Topology I and II: Lecture notes by Chris Wendl.
